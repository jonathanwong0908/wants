# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 25, 2025  
**Last updated:** June 25, 2025

Kloobel ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Wants mobile application ("Wants" or the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles, how that information is used, and the choices you have.

Wants is designed so your want lists and decisions stay on your device. We do not operate a backend server for Wants and we do not require you to create an account.

---

## Summary

- **Your want list data stays on your device** unless you choose to export it.
- **We do not sell your personal information.**
- **Subscriptions** are processed by Apple (and, when available, Google) through RevenueCat, our subscription management provider.
- **Push notifications** are scheduled locally on your device to remind you when a waiting period ends.

---

## Information we do not collect directly

We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or other contact information to use Wants.

We do not operate user accounts for Wants and we do not maintain a central database of your items, notes, prices, or decisions.

---

## Information stored on your device

When you use Wants, the App stores the following information locally on your device using on-device storage (SQLite):

- **Items you log**, including name, price, currency, waiting period, optional notes, status (waiting, skipped, or bought), and related timestamps
- **App settings**, such as default waiting period, currency preference, theme selection, and onboarding completion status
- **Notification identifiers** used to schedule and cancel reminders for individual items
- **Pro subscription status** cached locally for app functionality

This information is used only to provide App features, including tracking your waiting items, calculating your savings total, sending local reminders, and applying your preferences.

You can delete this information at any time using **Settings → Data → Clear all data** in the App. Clearing data permanently removes your items from the App on that device. Settings such as currency and default delay are not removed by this action unless otherwise stated in the App.

You may also **export your items to a CSV file** or **import items from a CSV file** using features in Settings. Export and import are initiated by you. We do not receive those files unless you choose to share them outside the App.

---

## Push notifications

If you allow notifications, Wants schedules reminders on your device to alert you when a waiting period ends. Notification content may include item details you entered, such as the item name and price.

Notification delivery is handled by your device's operating system (Apple Push Notification service on iOS, or the equivalent on Android). We do not send notifications from our own servers.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings. Wants can still be used without notifications, though you may need to open the App manually to see when items are ready to decide.

---

## Subscriptions and purchases

Wants offers an optional paid **Pro** tier with features such as unlimited active waiting items, custom waiting periods, and premium color themes.

When you purchase or restore a lifetime Pro unlock:

- **Payment processing** is handled by **Apple** (on iOS) or **Google** (on Android, when available), not by Kloobel.
- We use **RevenueCat, Inc.** to manage in-app purchases, validate entitlements, and restore purchases.

RevenueCat may receive information related to your purchase, such as:

- Purchase history and Pro unlock status
- Product identifiers
- An anonymous app user identifier generated by RevenueCat
- General device and store information needed to process purchases (for example, platform and locale)

We do not receive your full payment card details. Payment information is handled by Apple or Google under their respective privacy policies.

For more information about RevenueCat's data practices, see [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

---

## Information from your device

Wants may read limited device information to provide core functionality, including:

- **Locale / region** to suggest a default currency when you first use the App
- **Notification permission status** to show whether reminders are enabled
- **App version** shown in the About screen

We do not collect precise location data.

## Analytics

Wants does **not** currently use third-party analytics in the App. If we add optional analytics in a future version, we will update this Privacy Policy before or when that feature is enabled.

## How we use information

We use information only to:

- Provide and maintain App functionality
- Schedule and manage on-device reminders
- Process and restore Pro purchases
- Improve reliability and fix bugs
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your on-device want list data for advertising.

---

## How we share information

We do not sell your personal information.

We share information only in these situations:

- **Service providers** that help us operate subscriptions (RevenueCat) and app stores (Apple, Google), as needed to process purchases and entitlements
- **When you choose to share** exported CSV files or other content using your device's share sheet
- **Legal requirements**, if we believe disclosure is required by law or to protect rights, safety, or security

Because your want list data is stored locally, we generally cannot access it unless you contact us and voluntarily provide information.

---

## Data retention

- **On-device data** remains on your device until you delete it, uninstall the App, or clear data in Settings.
- **Purchase-related data** retained by Apple, Google, or RevenueCat is governed by their respective retention policies.

## Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of personal information.

Because Wants stores your want list locally, the fastest way to delete that data is through **Clear all data** in the App.

For subscription-related requests (such as purchase history managed by Apple or RevenueCat), you may also use:

- **iOS:** Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
- **Android:** Google Play → Payments & subscriptions
